Our Activities



"Garage Guru" Q&A Forum

Customers can ask questions about car maintenance, repairs, modifications, or anything car-related. Allow experienced customers or partnered mechanics to answer these questions, building a community around troubleshooting and car knowledge.

"Dream Garage" Car Configurator

Partner with car manufacturers or dealerships to offer a virtual car configurator tool. Customers can choose their desired make and model, then customize it with different colors, rims, interiors, and add-on features. This allows users to virtually build their dream car.

"Road Trip Builder" Planning Tool

Develop a "Road Trip Builder" where users can plan their next adventure. Customers can input their starting and ending points, desired route preferences (scenic, fastest, etc.), and even factor in stops for gas, food, attractions, or car maintenance needs.

Upcoming Event



Charity Car Wash Event

Partner with a local car wash and a car-related charity for a fundraising event. wHeelHub members can bring their cars for a discounted wash with all proceeds going to the chosen charity. Additional activities could include a raffle with car care product prizes, live music, or food trucks. This event promotes community engagement, car care awareness, and supporting a good cause.

Photo Contest: Classic Cars

Photography competition open to all wHeelHub members. Participants can submit photos of their classic cars (pre-1990s) or modern muscle cars (high-performance American vehicles). A panel of judges will select winners based on car aesthetics, photography quality, and overall creativity. Winners will receive prizes like car detailing packages, merchandise, or gift certificates for automotive parts stores.
